# Ubuntu Champions

![Ubuntu Champions Banner](media/image3.png)

## 🎮 About the Game

**Ubuntu Champions** is an educational action-adventure / game show simulator that celebrates the rich culture, history, and spirit of South Africa. Players compete in a high-energy game show, testing their knowledge of South African heritage, then embark on an open-world adventure to stop poachers and collect cultural artifacts.

Built with **Unity 3D** and scripted in **C#**, this game combines trivia, exploration, and action around the powerful theme of **Ubuntu** — *"I am because we are."*

---

## 🎯 Key Features

- **Unique Cultural Journey** – Test your knowledge of South African history, music (amapiano, kwaito), languages, and heroes
- **Dual Gameplay Experience** – Seamlessly blends a quiz-based TV game show (Level 1) with an open-world adventure (Level 2)
- **Spirit of Ubuntu** – Core narrative built around unity, community, and cultural preservation
- **Action for a Cause** – Track and stop poachers in a stylized South African bushveld setting
- **Vibrant Aesthetic** – Cartoonish art style inspired by SA street art and traditional patterns

## 🗺️ Game Levels

### Level 1: The Ubuntu Quiz Area (Studio Show)
- High-energy, neon-lit game-show studio
- 20 rapid-fire questions on South African culture
- Special rounds: Amapiano Beat Challenge & Rapid Fire Round
- **Duration:** 10 minutes

### Level 2: The Great SA Adventure (Open-World)
- Stylized open-world inspired by South African landmarks
- Track and stop poachers
- Collect cultural artifacts (Ubuntu Orbs)
- Optional wildlife rescue missions
- **Duration:** 20 minutes
